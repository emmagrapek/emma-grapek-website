The
Meat Project

↓
The Meat Project is intended to be a commentary on the everyday realities of a patriarchal society where women are sexually objectified. Society teaches men to hate women who are not fulfilling their ideologies of how women should be. That is- soft, submissive, and sweet. Sexy and alluring but not too forward. Virginal, but willing.

I asked people to participate in the project by designing their own T-Shirt based on a time they were cat-called or made sexually uncomfortable. I chose T-Shirts as a medium because they are commonly used as vessels to convey a message of self-expression or protest. The shirts are a protest against the sexual objectification of women- who have the right to walk down the street without being made uncomfortable.
